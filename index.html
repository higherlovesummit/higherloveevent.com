A Letter From Re Perez
Are you an entrepreneur, founder, or leader who is building something meaningful?
Like other high-performers, you're part of mastermind groups and business communities, such as Tony Robbins Platinum, EO, YPO, Vistage, or Tiger 21, where the conversations are elevated and the people are ambitious and driven.
Are you committed to your own “inner work,” personal growth, and spiritual development?
You've been part of meditative retreats and transformational experiences such as Landmark, Joe Dispenza, and Mindvalley, where you get out of your head and drop into your body, your heart, or your soul.
Are you bored and tired of the same old rooms for gay men?
The parties. The bars. The apps. Nothing wrong with any of these. They just don't seem to fit who you've become, or who you're becoming. You're looking for more presence over performance.

If that sounds like you, then I’d like to invite you to a first-of-its-kind room for gay men.

  • Where the other gay men can meet you at your level, intellectually, emotionally, and spiritually.
  • Where the business conversation and the intimate one happen in the same room.
  • Where the armor comes off, and you can just connect with others without having to prove anything.
  • Where every man in the room energizes you, inspires you, and challenges you.
  • Where you're fully seen for all of who you are.

For 20 years, I was looking for this room and I couldn't find it.

So I decided to build it.

I'm calling it Higher Love.

Why Be A Part Of Higher Love?

The fair question you're most likely going to ask:

What will you “get” by being a part of Higher Love?

The analytical part of you should ask it.

So picture a year from now.

Maybe a conversation with one of the men unlocks something in your business you'd been circling for months.

Maybe one of them becomes your next partner, in business or in life.

Maybe you'll get a client referral, an introduction to a key contact, or a connection to another kindred spirit.

I believe all that's possible when you curate a group of extraordinary gay men in the same room.

But if that's all you measure, you'll miss the larger return.

The men who do this don't just leave with a deal or a connection.

They leave with brothers.

A few men who know the whole picture of who you are.

The ones you can call at 11pm.

The ones who celebrate the win you can't explain to anyone else.

That's the return that compounds. Long after the deals come and go, you still have the room.

And the real “hook” is simpler than any of it. For the first time, a room where you don't edit yourself. Where you're seen, understood, and met for every part of who you are.

That's the invitation.

For the right man, this becomes the room he points to years from now as the one that changed everything.

Request your invitation to find out if this is your room.
