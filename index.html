The Three Questions

You've spent a career answering the question: “What do you do?” We ask three questions BEFORE any of that. Questions not typically asked in gay culture.

Who are you?

What's left when you're not hiding behind your title, accomplishments, or successes? Most rooms reward the performance. At Higher Love, we're interested in meeting the man underneath all of that.

How can we support you?

Whether or not you have a spouse or partner, every gay man can benefit from being part of a community where the men listen to you differently. Men who've built what you've built, carried what you carry, and can hold space for the hard things.

What is your legacy?

Few gay men are ever challenged to think about building a legacy. We were handed a script that ended at success and achievement. But a life that matters asks: what am I leaving behind for the next generation?
