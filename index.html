Finally, A Room Built For Gay Men Like You And Me.

A Letter From Re Perez
Question
Are you an entrepreneur, founder, or leader who is building something meaningful?
Like other high-performers, you're part of mastermind groups and business communities, such as Tony Robbins Business Mastery or Platinum, EO, YPO, or Vistage, where the conversations are elevated and the people are ambitious and driven.
Question
Are you committed to your own “inner work,” personal growth, and spiritual development?
You've been part of transformational experiences and meditative retreats, where you get out of your head and drop into your body and heart.
Question
Are you bored and tired of the same old rooms for gay men?
The parties. The bars. The apps. Nothing wrong with any of it. It just doesn't fit who you've become, or who you're becoming.

If that sounds like you, then I’d like to invite you to an invite-only room built for gay men like you (and me).

  • Where the other gay men can meet you at your level, intellectually, emotionally, and spiritually.
  • Where the business conversation and the intimate one happen in the same room, with the same brothers.
  • Where the armor comes off, and you connect with nothing left to prove.
  • Where every man in the room energizes you, inspires you, and challenges you to become your best.
  • Where you're fully seen for all of who you are.

Well, I went looking for that room.

I couldn't find it.

So I decided to build it.

I'm calling it Higher Love.

Higher Love sits where three worlds meet.

No one has put those three in one room for gay men. Until now.

First

Third
We'll commit to each other for one year. Long enough to get real value from the relationships formed in the group, but short enough that you don't have to commit to this forever (unless you want to).

Some of it will look like work. Some of it will look like nothing you've done before. All of it does one thing: it lets you be fully met, by other gay men who can actually see you.

If this sounds like something you'd like to be a part of, at the ground floor, then I invite you to request an invitation on this page. You'll answer a few questions and book a 45-minute call with me to see if this is the right room for you.

Why Be A Part Of Higher Love?

The fair question you're most likely going to ask: what will you “get” by being a part of Higher Love?

The analytical part of you should ask it. So picture a year from now.

Maybe a conversation with one of the brothers unlocks something in your business you'd been circling for months. Maybe one of them becomes your next partner, in business or in life. Maybe you'll get a client referral, an introduction to a key contact, or a connection to another kindred spirit.

I believe all that's possible when you curate a group of extraordinary gay men in the same room. But if that's all you measure, you'll miss the larger return.

The men who do this don't just leave with a deal or a connection. They leave with brothers. A few men who know the whole picture of who you are. The ones you can call at 11pm. The ones who celebrate the win you can't explain to anyone else.

That's the return that compounds. Long after the deals come and go, you still have the room.

And the real “hook” is simpler than any of it. For the first time, a room where you don't edit yourself. Where you're seen, understood, and met for every part of who you are.

That's the invitation.

For the right man, this becomes the room he points to years from now as the one that changed everything. I'm not looking for “bodies” to fill the room. I'm searching for gay men who are confident in who they are and ready to be in brotherhood with other gay men.

If you're that kind of man, I built this room for you (and for me too). Request your invitation to find out if this is your room.

Research consistently finds that gay men report far higher rates of severe loneliness than straight men, and that gap holds even at the top. The reason is simple: achievement and belonging are two different things. Most gay men learned to perform long before they learned to be seen. Higher Love exists for the question that lives underneath the achievement. You've built the success. Who are you beneath it?

High-achieving gay men are increasingly finding community in curated peer groups, small masterminds, and private retreats built around growth. These rooms bring together men who want depth, real friendship, and conversation that goes past the surface. The bar, the app, and the group chat each have their place. Higher Love is built for the deeper thing: being fully known by men who are walking the same road.
