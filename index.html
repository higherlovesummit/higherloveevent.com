A Letter From Re Perez

You're reading this because I personally reached out to you, or you were referred by someone I trust. Either way, it's not an accident.

If you're anything like me…

01

You're in the business and entrepreneurial rooms. EO, YPO, Vistage. The conversations are elevated, the people driven.

02

You're in the “inner work” rooms. The self-mastery programs, somatic retreats, and ceremonies. Where you drop out of your head and into your heart.

03

You're in the health, wellness, and longevity rooms. Not for vanity, but because you know your body is the vessel for everything you're building.

And in nearly every one of them, you're the only gay man. Or one of a few.

Not because you're hiding.
That's just the reality of the rooms you choose to be in.

And the rooms built for gay men? The parties. The bars. The apps. Nothing wrong with any of it. It just doesn't fit who you've become, or who you're becoming.

So you do the thing you've learned to do. You segment yourself depending on the room. And you walk out a little less “seen.”

What if you could be in a room with other gay men who can meet you at your level, intellectually, emotionally, and spiritually? Men who can talk business and strategy, and go just as deep on the things that really matter.

Would that be exciting to you?

I know it would be for me. Only problem is, when I went to look for that room, I couldn't find it.

So I decided to build it. I'm calling it Higher Love.

Higher Love sits where three worlds meet. A business mastermind. A personal growth experience. A health and wellness retreat.

No one has put those three in one room for gay men. Until now.

I'll personally interview up to 100 gay men and invite 12 to become founding members.

Here's what that means.

First, I'll host you and the other founding members this September in a private estate outside Austin. All you have to do is get yourself there. Everything else is handled. Nutritious, delicious meals from a private chef. Expert facilitators and guides who know how to hold a room of high performers.

Second, you'll help shape the culture of this room. We'll define together what brotherhood means for gay men like us. We'll explore what higher love looks like in each of our lives.

Third, we'll commit to each other for one year. Long enough to get real value from the relationships formed in the group, but short enough that you don't have to commit to this forever (unless you want to).

Some of it will look like work. Some of it will look like nothing you've done before. All of it does one thing: it lets you be fully met, by other gay men who can actually see you.

If this sounds like something you'd like to be a part of, at the ground floor, then I invite you to request an invitation on this page. You'll answer a few questions and book a 45-minute call with me to see if this is the right room for you.

Before you request your invitation… a little about me.

I've spent 25+ years as a brand strategist helping companies and personal brands become a category of one. I've worked with Fortune 500 companies, as well as thousands of CEOs, founders, and entrepreneurs across numerous industries.

I've spent decades immersed in personal development, as a participant, a coach, and a program leader. I've also integrated that work to create masterminds and retreats.

The throughline is always the same. The right people in the right room change everything. I know how to find those people, and I know how to build the room around them.

The fair question you're most likely going to ask: what will you get by being a part of Higher Love?

The analytical part of you should ask it. So picture a year from now.

Maybe a conversation with one of the brothers unlocks something in your business you'd been circling for months. Maybe one of them becomes your next partner, in business or in life. Maybe you'll get a client referral, an introduction to a key contact, or a connection to another kindred spirit.

I believe all that's possible when you curate a group of extraordinary gay men in the same room. But if that's all you measure, you'll miss the larger return.

The men who do this don't just leave with a deal or a connection. They leave with brothers. A few men who know the whole picture of who you are. The ones you can call at 11pm. The ones who celebrate the win you can't explain to anyone else.

That's the return that compounds. Long after the deals come and go, you still have the room.

And the real “hook” is simpler than any of it. For the first time, a room where you don't edit yourself. Where you're seen, understood, and met for every part of who you are.

That's the invitation.

For the right man, this becomes the room he points to years from now as the one that changed everything. I'm not looking for “bodies” to fill the room. I'm searching for gay men who are confident in who they are and ready to be in brotherhood with other gay men.

If you're that kind of man, I built this room for you (and for me too). So request your invitation, and let's find out together if this is your room.
