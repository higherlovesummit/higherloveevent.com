Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

Who We Are

Higher Love is operated by Category of One Branding LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website higherloveevent.com and interact with our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out forms on our website. This includes:

Application Form: First name, last name, email address, professional role, LinkedIn profile URL, referral source, and your responses to our qualifying questions.

Nomination Form: Your name, your email address, the nominated person’s name, and the nominated person’s email address. By submitting a nomination, you represent that you have a reasonable basis to believe the nominated individual would welcome being contacted about Higher Love.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to evaluate applications for the Higher Love gathering, to contact you regarding your application or nomination, to schedule and facilitate conversations with Re Perez, to send event related communications, and to improve our website and services.

Third Party Services

We use the following third party services that may receive your data: Google Fonts for typography rendering, Calendly for booking conversations, and our application processing infrastructure hosted on Cloudflare Workers. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. If you would like your data deleted, please contact us at the email below.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Our website is served over HTTPS and our data processing infrastructure uses industry standard security practices. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for functionality. We use Google Fonts which may set cookies for font delivery optimization. We do not use analytics tracking cookies or advertising cookies at this time.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at applications@higherloveevent.com.
